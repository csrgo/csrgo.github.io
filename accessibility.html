Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: May 26, 2025

Last Updated: May 28, 2025

At CSRGO Tools (tools.csrgo.com), we are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including those with disabilities. We aim to create a website that is accessible, usable, and inclusive for everyone, regardless of ability or technology.

1. Our Commitment

We strive to make our website conform to globally recognized accessibility standards such as:

  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA
  • Best practices recommended by India's Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (where applicable)

2. Scope and Nature of the Site

This site is entirely static and front-end based, operating within your browser only.

  • It uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to offer a variety of free tools (e.g., text utilities, image converters, etc.).
  • We do not collect or store any personal data and do not use back-end servers.

We aim to design our user interfaces (UI) to be:

  • Keyboard accessible
  • Readable with screen readers
  • Compatible with zoom and text scaling
  • Responsive across devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones

3. Measures We Are Taking

We are actively working on:

  • Fixing low contrast issues for better readability
  • Adding missing accessible labels to form fields and buttons
  • Providing descriptive alternative text for images and icons
  • Improving keyboard navigation throughout the site
  • Declaring proper page language and document titles
  • Using semantic HTML for better screen reader support

4. Limitations

Due to the nature of our static, browser-only architecture, there may be some limitations in dynamic accessibility enhancements (like real-time ARIA live regions or interactive WAI-ARIA features). Some elements may not yet be fully optimized for assistive technologies. However, we are continually refining our design to improve accessibility.

5. Third-Party Content

Some content, such as Google Ads or embedded widgets, may not be fully controlled or accessible. We encourage users to refer to the accessibility policies of such third-party services.

6. Feedback and Assistance

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of CSRGO Tools. If you encounter any issues or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • contact.csrgo@gmail.com
  • csrgo.com

We will do our best to respond within a reasonable time and work to address any accessibility barriers.

7. Compliance Status

This website is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. Partial conformance means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard, especially due to contrast issues, missing labels, and keyboard navigation gaps. We are aware of these and are actively working to resolve them.
